How to Make a Paper Doll Chain
  1. Cut a long strip of paper.
  2. Fold the paper like an accordion and get a rectangle.
  3. Draw the outline of the doll in the center of the rectangle.
  4. Cut out the doll.
  5. As you slowly unroll the strip, you should end up with a chain of dolls holding hands.
  6. Decorate the finished chain.
